Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Important: This refund policy applies to all purchases made through vibeLink, including subscriptions and one-time purchases.

1. General Refund Policy

All payments made through vibeLink are generally final and non-refundable, except in specific circumstances outlined below or as required by applicable law.

2. Subscription Refunds

Our Subscription Plans:

  • vibeLink Plus: ₹299 per month
  • vibeLink Gold: ₹499 per month
  • vibeLink Platinum: ₹799 per month

2.1 No Refunds for Used Services

  • Subscriptions are charged monthly in advance
  • No refunds for partial months of service
  • You retain access until the end of your billing period after cancellation
  • Cancellation prevents future charges but doesn't refund current period

2.2 Exceptions for Refunds

We may provide refunds in the following cases:

  • Technical Issues: If you cannot access the service due to technical problems on our end
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly or multiple times
  • Unauthorized Charges: If someone else used your payment method without permission
  • Legal Requirements: Where refunds are required by local consumer protection laws

3. One-time Purchase Refunds

3.1 Boost Purchases

  • Super Likes (₹99): Non-refundable once used
  • Profile Boost (₹149): Non-refundable once activated
  • Spotlight (₹199): Non-refundable once activated

3.2 Unused Boost Refunds

Refunds may be considered for unused boosts within 24 hours of purchase if:

  • The boost was purchased by mistake
  • Technical issues prevented the boost from working
  • You contact support before using the boost

4. Refund Request Process

4.1 How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact our support team at support@vibelink.app
  2. Include your account email and reason for refund request
  3. Provide transaction details (date, amount, payment method)
  4. Submit request within 7 days of the charge

4.2 Required Information

  • Account email address
  • Transaction ID or receipt
  • Date and amount of purchase
  • Detailed reason for refund request
  • Screenshots of any technical issues (if applicable)

4.3 Processing Time

  • We review refund requests within 5 business days
  • Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Bank processing may take additional 3-5 business days

5. Platform-Specific Policies

5.1 App Store (iOS) Purchases

  • Purchases made through Apple App Store are subject to Apple's refund policy
  • Contact Apple Support directly for App Store purchase refunds
  • We cannot process refunds for App Store transactions

5.2 Google Play Store (Android) Purchases

  • Purchases made through Google Play are subject to Google's refund policy
  • Contact Google Play Support for Google Play purchase refunds
  • We cannot process refunds for Google Play transactions

5.3 Direct App Purchases (UPI/Card)

  • Purchases made directly through our app are subject to this policy
  • Contact our support team for direct purchase refunds
  • Processed through Razorpay and Stripe payment systems

6. Chargeback Policy

  • Chargebacks may result in account suspension
  • Contact us before initiating a chargeback
  • We will work to resolve issues without chargebacks
  • Disputed chargebacks may incur additional fees

7. Consumer Rights

This policy doesn't limit your rights under applicable consumer protection laws, including:

  • Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (India)
  • Local state consumer protection laws
  • Rights to refunds for defective or mis-sold services
  • Cooling-off periods where applicable

8. Account Termination

  • No refunds if your account is terminated for policy violations
  • Voluntary account deletion doesn't qualify for refunds
  • Remaining subscription time is forfeited upon termination

9. Special Circumstances

9.1 Medical/Emergency Situations

We may consider refunds in extraordinary circumstances such as:

  • Serious illness or hospitalization
  • Death of account holder
  • Military deployment
  • Other documented emergencies

9.2 Required Documentation

Special circumstance refunds require appropriate documentation and are reviewed case-by-case.

10. Changes to Refund Policy

  • We may update this policy from time to time
  • Changes apply to future purchases, not existing ones
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app

11. Contact for Refunds

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

Tips for Avoiding Refund Issues:

  • Carefully review subscription terms before purchasing
  • Cancel subscriptions before renewal if you don't want to continue
  • Contact support immediately for any technical issues
  • Keep receipts and transaction records
