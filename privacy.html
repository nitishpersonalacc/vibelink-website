Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile information (name, age, photos, bio, interests)
  • Account credentials (email, password)
  • Communication preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment partners)
  • Messages and interactions with other users

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Usage information (app features used, time spent, interactions)
  • Location information (with your consent, for matching and event suggestions)
  • Log information (IP address, browser type, referring URLs)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our dating services
  • To show you potential matches based on your preferences
  • To suggest local events and communities
  • To process payments and subscriptions
  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To ensure safety and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information and photos are visible to other users as part of our matching service.

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Payment processors (Stripe, Razorpay) for transaction processing
  • Cloud storage providers for data hosting
  • Analytics providers for app improvement
  • Customer support tools

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a common format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing

6. Location Information

We use location information to:

  • Show you potential matches nearby
  • Suggest local events and activities
  • Improve our recommendation algorithms

You can control location sharing through your device settings.

7. Payments and Billing

Payment information is processed securely by our payment partners (Stripe and Razorpay). We do not store your complete credit card information on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

vibeLink is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred and processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
